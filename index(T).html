About Our Founder

Mr. Krishna Trivedi brings a wealth of knowledge and a unique perspective to teaching Physics. With a solid foundation from Hindu College, Delhi University, and Kanpur University, he has dedicated 5 years to teaching, making complex concepts understandable and engaging for students.

His professional journey includes valuable experience as a Former Byjus and Internshala professional, where he honed his skills in delivering effective and impactful educational content. As the Founder of Together We-Dream, an initiative that connects professionals with students for career guidance, Mr. Trivedi has mentored over 7,000 students. Currently serving as a PGT Physics in a CBSE affiliated School, he is passionate about fostering a deep understanding of Physics and guiding students towards academic excellence and beyond.
